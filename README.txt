About the game
There are two decks, on face down and one face up.
The first player will first draw from the face down pile and discard one card, keeping only 4 cards total.
After players may choose to draw from the face up pile or face down pile.
Once a player as 4 cards that equal 23 or 4 of the same suit cards, they win.
This game is only a simulation with a specifc algorithm to follow

*Note logic error when discarding.
